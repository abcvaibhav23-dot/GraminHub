Privacy Note

We keep privacy controls simple and practical. This note explains how login, booking, and search-related data is handled on GraminHub.

1. Account data

Name, email, and encrypted password are stored for authentication and role-based access (Guest/User/Supplier/Admin).

2. Booking and supplier interactions

Booking requests, ratings, and call logs are stored to run the marketplace workflow and support dispute resolution.

3. Search keywords and SEO

We maintain a broad keyword library to improve discoverability on search platforms. This keyword corpus is generic and not tied to personal user identity.

4. User disclaimer (buyer-side risk controls)

  • Users must provide genuine identity/contact details when placing booking requests.
  • Fake bookings, false payment claims, abusive calls/messages, or impersonation are treated as misuse.
  • On verified misuse, user access may be blocked and related logs may be shared with legal authorities when required by law.

5. Buyer disclaimer

  • Buyers must verify supplier quality, material specs, and price before payment.
  • GraminHub acts as a discovery and booking platform and does not guarantee third-party supplier performance.
  • For large orders, buyers should use written confirmations and GST/legal invoices where applicable.

6. Supplier disclaimer

  • Suppliers are responsible for lawful listings, correct pricing, and truthful availability.
  • Supplier contact data shown on the platform is intended only for business booking communication.
  • Repeated fraudulent activity, spam, or abuse may lead to profile block/removal by admin.
